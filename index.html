Welcome to my Page

My name is Azeemat. I am a student at AltschoolAfrica who's learning to be a cloud engineer. As a lover of animals, I own two bunnies as pets, and I hope to take in more homeless animals when I get myself a larger space.

I enjoy having fun with my friends; chatting and seeing new places. I also love the scenery and enjoy walking by the sea beach.

Learning at Altschool is gradually making me become a tech enthusiasst. While I'm not necessarily having the smoothest of rides, I understand that, as is with most learning changing processes in life, this will pass and I'm trusting the process 100%. However, my goal for this course is quite simple; I want to be a world-class cloud engineer in few months to come!

I am a Microbiology graduate of the Federal University, Oye-Ekiti.

During my National Youth Service year, I served at the Nigerian Armed Forces Ressetlement Centre as a Co-supervisor in the Agric Wing. I have also worked as a Covid-19 vaccination personnel.
